EcoLoop Marketplace Overview

EcoLoop Marketplace is Kenya's leading platform for buying and selling secondhand goods. We connect eco-conscious buyers with responsible sellers, promoting sustainability through circular economy principles. Our platform ensures secure transactions, verified sellers, and quality products across multiple categories.


Whether you're looking to declutter your home, find unique items at great prices, or contribute to environmental conservation, EcoLoop Marketplace provides the perfect solution for sustainable commerce in Kenya.

How Buyers Shop on EcoLoop

1

Browse Products

Explore our extensive collection of secondhand items across categories like electronics, fashion, home goods, and sports equipment. Use filters to find exactly what you need.

2

Review Details

View detailed product descriptions, high-quality images, seller ratings, and customer reviews. Check product condition, specifications, and pricing information.

3

Add to Cart

Select items you love and add them to your cart. You can continue shopping or proceed to checkout when ready. Save items to wishlist for later consideration.

4

Secure Payment

Complete your purchase using M-Pesa or other secure payment methods. Our payment system ensures your financial information is protected at all times.

5

Receive Delivery

Track your order and receive your items through our secure delivery network. Inspect products and confirm satisfaction with our quality guarantee.

6

Rate & Review

Share your experience with the community. Rate products and sellers to help others make informed decisions and improve the marketplace.

How Sellers Upload Products

1

Register as Seller

2

Take Photos

Capture high-quality images of your products from multiple angles. Show details, condition, and any unique features to attract buyers.

3

Write Description

Provide detailed product descriptions including condition, specifications, usage history, and any defects. Be honest and transparent.

4

Set Pricing

Research market prices and set competitive rates. Consider product condition, brand, and market demand when determining your prices.

5

Submit for Approval

Upload your listing for admin review. Our team verifies product details and ensures compliance with marketplace guidelines.

6

Go Live & Sell

Once approved, your product becomes visible to buyers. Manage inquiries, negotiate offers, and complete sales through our platform.

NyumbaSure Housing Overview

NyumbaSure is EcoLoop's trusted housing platform that connects tenants with verified real estate agents and property listings across Kenya. We ensure safe, transparent, and reliable housing transactions through our comprehensive verification system and escrow services.


Whether you're looking for a rental property or planning to buy a home, NyumbaSure provides verified listings, trustworthy agents, and secure payment processing to make your housing journey smooth and worry-free.

How Tenants Find Houses

1

Set Location

Choose your preferred location, neighborhood, and area. Filter by proximity to work, schools, and amenities that matter to you.

2

Apply Filters

Use advanced filters for price range, property type, bedrooms, bathrooms, and specific amenities like parking, security, and utilities.

3

View Properties

Browse verified property listings with detailed photos, virtual tours, floor plans, and comprehensive property descriptions.

4

Schedule Viewing

Book property viewings with verified agents at your convenience. Choose from available time slots and get instant confirmations.

5

Negotiate Terms

Discuss rental terms, payment schedules, and any special requirements with the agent through our secure messaging system.

6

Sign Agreement

Review and sign digital lease agreements with legal protections. Use our escrow service for secure deposit and rent payments.

Agent Verification Process

1

Agent Registration

Real estate agents register with personal details, business information, and professional credentials including licenses and certifications.

2

Document Verification

Our team verifies all submitted documents including ID cards, business registrations, and professional licenses through official channels.

3

Background Check

Comprehensive background checks including criminal records, business history, and previous client reviews and complaints.

4

Business Verification

Physical verification of business premises and confirmation of active real estate operations and client portfolio.

5

Rating System

Initial rating based on verification results. Agents build reputation through successful transactions and client feedback.

6

Verified Badge

Approved agents receive verified badges and enhanced visibility on the platform with priority listing placement.

M-Pesa Escrow System

Our M-Pesa escrow system ensures secure financial transactions for both rentals and property purchases. Here's how it works:


Secure Holding

Funds are held securely in escrow until all conditions are met and both parties are satisfied with the transaction.

Verification Release

Funds are only released after property inspection, document verification, and confirmation of satisfactory conditions.

Dispute Protection

Built-in dispute resolution mechanism ensures fair handling of any conflicts with neutral mediation.

Transaction Records

Complete digital trail of all transactions with receipts and timestamps for legal and tax purposes.

Safety & Reporting System

Report Suspicious Activity

Easy reporting system for fraudulent listings, fake agents, or suspicious behavior with immediate response.

Identity Protection

Personal information is protected and only shared with verified parties after mutual agreement.

Virtual Tours

Safe virtual property tours available for initial viewing before physical visits with verified agents.

Emergency Support

24/7 emergency hotline for immediate assistance during property viewings or transactions.

Legal Support

Access to legal assistance for contract review and dispute resolution through partnered law firms.

Document Verification

All property documents, titles, and ownership papers are verified with government registries.
