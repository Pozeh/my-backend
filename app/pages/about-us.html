Our Story

EcoLoop Kenya was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a sustainable marketplace that benefits both people and the planet. What started as a small initiative to promote secondhand shopping has grown into Kenya's most comprehensive platform for sustainable commerce and verified housing services.


Our journey began when we noticed the growing need for a trusted platform where Kenyans could buy and sell pre-owned items while also accessing verified housing services. We saw an opportunity to combine technology with sustainability, creating a platform that not only facilitates commerce but also contributes to environmental conservation.


Today, EcoLoop Kenya serves thousands of users across the country, helping them save money, reduce waste, and make more sustainable choices. We're proud to be at the forefront of Kenya's sustainable commerce revolution, and we're committed to continuing our mission of creating a greener, more sustainable future for all Kenyans.

What We Do

EcoLoop Kenya operates two main platforms designed to promote sustainability and trust in commerce:


EcoLoop Marketplace: Our flagship platform for buying and selling secondhand goods. From electronics and fashion to home goods and sports equipment, we provide a secure environment for sustainable shopping. Every transaction on our platform contributes to reducing waste and promoting a circular economy.


NyumbaSure Housing: Our verified housing platform that connects tenants with trusted real estate agents and property listings. We ensure that all agents are thoroughly verified and all listings are authentic, providing a safe and reliable housing marketplace for Kenyans.


Both platforms are designed with sustainability, security, and user experience at their core, ensuring that every interaction is positive and contributes to our mission of creating a more sustainable Kenya.
